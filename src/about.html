About Me

I am a student studying Full Stack Web Development at Weber State University. However, I wasn't always interested in web design. As I took steps to enter college, I changed my major a couple times but wasn't happy with it.

I remembered taking a Web Development class in high school that I found enjoyable, as I was able to manipulate a website's elements through code and use my keen attention to detail to spot any syntax errors. I also liked the variety of possibilites for elements that could form a website.

I promptly changed my major to Web Development and have been enjoying using both my creative and logical sides to create wonderful websites!
