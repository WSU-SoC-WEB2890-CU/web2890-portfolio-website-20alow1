Cars at Night

Role: Photographer & Editor

Technologies: DSLR Camera, Photoshop

Time to Complete: 2 days

Description

I took this photo for an assignment in my photography class. The goal was to photograph a pleasing photo while involving some creative light elements.

I was required to take the photo at night, and experiment with longer shutter speeds to capture more light in the darker environment. This photo was the result of seeking out a location with moving lights to create the streaking light effect in the darkness.

Something that works well in this photo is not just the streaks of light, but that there are different colors of streaks to give variety to the photo. When strategically placed on a website, it helps catch the user's eye and guides them to the next thing they should look at.
