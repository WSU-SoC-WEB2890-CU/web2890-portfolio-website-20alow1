Electrol

Role: Designer & Developer

Technologies: HTML, CSS, Photoshop

Time to Complete: 1 month

Description

Electrol is a fictitious electric vehicle company that I imagined for my final website project in Web 1400. Electrol sells electric cars, bikes, and even boats. My goal for the website was to create something non-traditional that stands out from other websites.

The professor had some requirements such as creating a fake company to design a website for, along with having 5 pages on the website. I created the company's logo and all the content in the webpage, as well as a mockup of my design for the website.

One of the first things you notice about this website is that the navigation is on the left side of the screen, instead of the usual top. The goal was to create a different look for the website that piques people's interest and encourages them to explore the website more. It also allows the users to access any part of the navigation at any time, with a different style than just having the navbar fixed at the top.
